# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Local residents of Foggia (and surrounding province) needing eyewear, sunglasses, vision exams, or hearing checks. Mostly discover via Google search/maps or word of mouth, land on this single page on mobile, and decide whether to call. Includes older clientele (hearing screening, presbyopia) — readability and clarity matter more than trend-chasing.

## Product Purpose

A single-page marketing site for Ottica Vedere, a 35-year-old family-run optical and hearing shop in Foggia. Exists to build trust fast and convert visits into phone calls (primary goal) or in-store visits. Success = a visitor calls 0881 714001 or opens directions within the first screen or two.

## Brand Personality

Trusted / heritage, premium / refined, warm / personal. Voice: confident but never corporate — Giuliano is named directly, reviews are personal, tone reads like a respected local expert rather than a franchise. Should feel like a boutique you'd trust with your eyes and your parents' hearing, not a stock optical chain.

## Anti-references

No specific named site to avoid, but must not read as a generic website-builder / stock SaaS-landing-page template. Avoid corporate-chain-optician coldness; avoid anything that undercuts the "35 years, one trusted owner" story.

## Design Principles

- Trust through specificity: named owner, named streets, real reviews — never generic stock-photo confidence.
- One clear next action per screen: the phone call is the goal, don't compete with it.
- Legible over trendy: older clientele reads this page; clarity wins over cleverness every time contrast or type size is in question.
- Premium restraint: gold/navy heritage palette carries the luxury signal, not decoration for its own sake.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum, with extra care on body-text contrast and touch-target size given an older user base (hearing/vision clinic clientele). Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` (already implemented). No specific named anti-reference beyond generic templates.
